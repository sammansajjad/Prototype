Who We Are

Human Appeal Pakistan is the dedicated national chapter of Human Appeal international, established to support vulnerable communities across the provinces of Sindh, Punjab, Balochistan, and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

Guided by our faith-inspired values of duty, transparency, and compassion, we implement long-term developmental projects alongside rapid emergency relief during floods, earthquakes, and winter seasons.

Our goal is not just to provide immediate support, but to construct sustainable infrastructure—like solar water wells and vocational training centers—that allows families to break the cycle of poverty and build self-sufficient futures.
